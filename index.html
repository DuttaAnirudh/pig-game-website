How To Play

  • 2 players, 3 buttons: 'Roll Dice', 'Hold', 'New Game'
  • Roll Dice button adds numbers to your temporary score until you roll a '1'
  • Clicking 'Hold' button adds your temporary score to your main score, and switches players
  • Rolling a '1' resets your temporary score, and it's the next player's turn
  • First to reach a main score of 100 wins
  • To start a new game, Press "NEW GAME".
